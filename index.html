FAQ

Why is staying hydrated so important?

Staying hydrated is key to energy, balance, and overall health. It boosts brain function, aids digestion, keeps skin healthy, and enhances performance.

How much water should I drink each day?

Aim for about 8 cups (2L) of water daily, though needs vary by activity, climate, and health. Our app helps you stay hydrated!

What are Aqua Points, and how do I earn them?

Earn Aqua Points by completing hydration challenges, maintaining streaks, and leveling up your hydration rank. unlock rewards as you stay consistent!

How does the app help me develop a hydration habit?

Our app turns hydration into a game! Complete challenges, earn rewards, and stay motivated with reminders and community support.

Is the app free to use?

Yes! Our app is free, offering tracking, challenges, rewards, and community chat. Premium features are optional for extra perks.

What makes your app different from other hydration apps?

Unlike other apps, we make hydration fun with challenges, rewards, a community chat, and engaging comic-based lessons!

How does the community feature work?

Our app includes a chatroom where you can share your hydration progress, swap tips and stay motivated with other like-minded individuals.

What if I miss a day or fall off track?

No worries! Hydration is a journey, and we’re here to support you. Our app’s reminders, challenges, and supportive community help you stay on track.
